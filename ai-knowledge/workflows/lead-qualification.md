# Lead Qualification Workflow

## BANT Framework
Qualify leads using Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline.

### Budget
- Does the prospect have budget allocated for this type of solution?
- If unknown: ask during initial call, log as note on contact
- Minimum viable deal size: configurable per workspace

### Authority
- Is the contact the decision maker?
- If not: identify and connect with the economic buyer
- Champions vs. decision makers — track both on the deal

### Need
- What problem are they trying to solve?
- Log pain points in opportunity notes
- Map to product capabilities

### Timeline
- When are they looking to implement?
- <30 days = Hot lead → prioritize
- 30-90 days = Warm lead → nurture sequence
- >90 days = Cold lead → low-touch follow-up

## Scoring
| Criteria | Points |
|---|---|
| Budget confirmed | 25 |
| Decision maker identified | 20 |
| Clear need articulated | 30 |
| Timeline <90 days | 25 |

- Score 80-100: Move to Screening immediately
- Score 50-79: Flag for sales rep review
- Score <50: Add to nurture sequence

## Agent Behavior
- When creating a new contact with company association, prompt for BANT qualification
- Calculate and display lead score when asked
- Suggest next action based on score bracket
- Never move a deal to Proposal without minimum score of 70
